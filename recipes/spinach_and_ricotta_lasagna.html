Spinach and Ricotta Lasagna

Description

A lighter, vegetarian take that layers tender pasta with creamy ricotta, saut√©ed spinach, and a bright tomato sauce, finishing with a golden mozzarella-Parmesan top. The balance of garlicky greens and mellow cheeses keeps the texture soft and the flavor clean, while baking melds the layers into neat, sliceable squares

Ingredients

  1. Lasagna sheets
  2. Fresh spinach (or frozen, thawed and squeezed dry)
  3. Ricotta cheese
  4. Mozzarella cheese
  5. Garlic and onion
  6. Olive oil
  7. Crushed tomatoes or marinara sauce
  8. Salt, pepper, nutmeg (optional)
  9. Fresh basil or parsley (optional)
